Puzzle Massive Has Shutdown…

Until further notice, the site will remain down indefinitely.

I developed Puzzle Massive as something fun to build in my spare time. The source code for it will remain hosted at GitHub, but I will no longer be doing much with version 2 of that codebase (the live site was running version 2.12.4). I plan to continue development of Puzzle Massive, but will be focusing effort on a new version 3. This new version may or may not be backwards compatible with the data from version 2. This means that any puzzles or player account data that was on version 2 may not transfer over.
